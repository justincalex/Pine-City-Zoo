ANIMALS MAP PLACES

ELEPHANTS

The largest land animal, known for its intelligence, strong tusks, and deep family bonds.

GEMSBOK

A striking antelope with long, sharp horns, built for speed and survival in harsh desert landscapes.

GIRAFFES

The tallest land animal, with a long neck, spotted coat, and a graceful stride that reaches the treetops.

GORILLAS

A powerful yet gentle giant, known for intelligence, strong family bonds, and expressive eyes.

KOALAS

A fluffy, tree-dwelling marsupial, known for its calm nature, strong grip, and love of eucalyptus leaves.

LIONS

The fearless king of the savanna, known for its strength, majestic mane, and powerful roar.

MONKEYS

A lively and intelligent climber, known for its playful antics, curious nature, and expressive face.

PANDAS

A cuddly black-and-white bamboo lover, known for its playful nature and iconic fluffy appearance.

WARTHOGS

A rugged and resilient wild pig, known for its tusks, speedy sprints, and tough survival instincts.
